## Official plugins and extensions

The plugins and extensions maintained by the jsPsych team are documented in the [Reference](../reference/plugins/html-keyboard-response.md) section.

## Community plugins and extensions

Community-contributed plugins and extensions live in the [`jspsych-contrib`](https://github.com/jspsych/jspsych-contrib) repository. Browse the repository's `packages/` directory to see what's available.

## Community timelines

Reusable, parameterized procedures live in the [`jspsych-timelines`](https://github.com/jspsych/jspsych-timelines) repository.

## Don't see what you need?

If nothing fits, you can [build your own](index.mdx) — and [share it](publish-and-share.md) so the next person doesn't have to.
